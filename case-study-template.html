Positive Affirmation Page

The project began with the objective to create a CSS style sheet and 10-15 HTML templates for teachers to create new courses with and students to access course material through. The templates will be used by teachers to create courses through the Brightspace learning platform. My job was to design and develop templates for teachers to input content into.

SCOPE:

Through usability testing, the scope of the product shifted to the design and development of 2-3 HTML layouts, 1 CSS sheet, and 1 page of copy-and-pastable components, which teachers can copy into their course at their convenience.

I hope you're curious about why the shift (such exciting research findings!!)! Don't worry, I won't keep you in suspense for long!
