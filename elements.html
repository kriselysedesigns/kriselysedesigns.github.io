Positive Affirmation Page

The project began with the objective to create a CSS style sheet and 10-15 html templates for teachers to create new coursed with and students to access course material through. The templates will be used by teachers to create courses through the Brightspace learning platform. My job was to design and develop templates for teachers to input content into.

SCOPE:

Through usability testing, the scope of the product shifted to the design and development of 2-3 html layouts, 1 css sheet, and 1 page of copy-and-pastable components, which teachers can coping into their course at their convenience.

I hope you're curious about why the shift (such exciting research findings!!)! Don't worry, I won't keep you in suspense for long!

Who's the User?

PAIN POINTS:

Teacher

Tedious copying and pasting during summer vacation
Lack of instruction on how to operate new template. Frustration -wasted time.
Fear of losing information and course data in transfer and revision.

aDMIN

Fear of being misrepresented by sub par product.
Pressure to learning new system before delegating to teachers.
Fear that the transfer in learning platform will lose business or overwhelm students.

STUDENT

Disinterest in course material
Confusion and frustration of learning new online learning platform.
Fear of missing an assignment or quiz
Overwhelmed with amount of information.

PARENT

Overwhelmed - wil I be able to troubleshoot with time difference?
Stuck when unable to find course requirements.
Annoyed when help is not available immediately if course produces error.

USER GOALS:

Teacher

As a teacher trying to engage students online, I need an engaging and flexible system to grab student's attention, as well as fun content because I need students to be engaged in content and pass courses.

Administrator

As an owner of the company that I built from the ground up, I need our online learning platform reflect our reputation of being ahead of the curve and the best in the industry because I want to keep the respect of our teachers and students, as well as other professionals in the industry.

Student

As a student operating on opposite time zones as my teacher, I want my online courses to be clear and easy to use because learning chemistry is overwhelming enough and I only have so much patience for confusion.

Parent

As a parent invested in my child's education, I need my child's coursed to be accessible on tablet and phone because, with 3 other kids, I need my child to be able to work on course work while waiting at their sibling's soccer practice.
